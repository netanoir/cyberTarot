— Toward A New Religious Tradition, Marcelitte the Third

The tarot first known as trionfi and later as tarocchi or tarock is a pack of playing cards, used from the mid-15th century in various parts of the world. In the late 18th century, some tarot packs began to be used as a tool for divination via tarot card reading and cartomancy leading to custom packs developed for such occult purposes.
